Introducing The City Awards

www.thecityawards.com is a place where you can vote for London's Best Burger!

There's no login required, simply drag the cute little burger icon onto your favourite place to vote!

Tehcnologies Used

This is my first experiment with React, which combined with Browserify does an amazing job pre-rendering all the views combined with the data model on the server, then lets the JavaScript on the client take over!

Please vote, and follow @thecityawards :-)

Have you been to any of these places? I would love for you to vote for the Best Burger in London, it will take 10 seconds - I promise!

You can follow @thecityawards on Twitter, we'll be giving away free burgers so stay tuned!
